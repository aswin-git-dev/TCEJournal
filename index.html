Aim & Scope

The Thiagarajar Journal of Engineering, Science, Design, and Technology (TJESDT) aims to be an interdisciplinary platform for cutting-edge research in engineering, applied sciences, technological innovation, and design. The journal is focused on fostering collaboration and sharing impactful research findings from all over India.

Topics covered include Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics, Computer Science, Environmental Engineering, AI, Robotics, Quantum Computing, Renewable Energy, and more.

Editorial Board

The editorial board consists of leading researchers, professionals, and academics who maintain the integrity and quality of the journal. They ensure that all submitted articles undergo a rigorous peer-review process to meet the highest standards of academic publishing.

Submission Guidelines

Authors wishing to submit to TJESDT should follow these key guidelines:

  • Manuscripts must be written in English.
  • Maximum word count: 7,500 words.
  • APA citation style must be used for references.

Publication Types

TJESDT publishes the following types of content:

  • Original Research Articles
  • Review Articles
  • Case Studies/Practices
  • Special Issues
